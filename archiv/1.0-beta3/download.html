Automatically with Maven

  1. Add the Settings4j Repository to your pom.xml:
    <repositories>
        <repository>
                <id>settings4j</id>
                <url>http://settings4j.sourceforge.net/m2repo</url>
        </repository>
</repositories>
  2. Add the Settings4j Dependency to your pom.xml:
    <dependency>
        <groupId>org.settings4j</groupId>
        <artifactId>settings4j</artifactId>
        <version>1.0-beta3</version>
</dependency>
